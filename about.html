About New Grace JS

New Grace JS is a global charity committed to addressing the critical need for clean water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) in underserved communities worldwide. Founded in 2010, we've worked across 35 countries to implement sustainable solutions that transform lives.

Our approach combines infrastructure development with community education and empowerment. We believe that access to clean water is a fundamental human right and the foundation for health, education, and economic development.

With a team of local experts and international specialists, we tailor solutions to each community's specific needs, ensuring long-term success and local ownership of water projects.

Our Vision

To create a world where every person has access to safe water, sanitation, and hygiene, enabling healthy, empowered, and sustainable communities.

Our Impact

  • Over 5,000 community members reached
  • 1,200 school children benefited
  • 20+ local schools supported
  • Dozens of water and sanitation projects completed
